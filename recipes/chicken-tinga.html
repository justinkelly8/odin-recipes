Chicken Tinga


Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (consider bone-in)
  • 2-5 canned chiptole peppers
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tsp kosher salt
  • freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Mexican oregano
  • chili powder
  • one whole bay leaf
  • smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Roughly chop onion, smash garlic cloves
  2. Add onion, garlic, chipotle peppers, chicken & spices to a crock pot
  3. Let it braise on low for 6 hours or high for 3 hours
  4. Remove the chicken to cool, set crock pot to high
  5. Use an immersion blender to turn braising liquid into a smooth sauce
  6. Shred the chicken (with your hands or two forks) and return it to the sauce
  7. Serve chicken as part of nachos, tacos, toastados, quesadillas, or enchiladas.
